Courses We Offer

Vignan’s Nirula offers a wide range of programs including B.Tech, MCA, and MBA, designed to build strong academic foundations and industry-ready skills.

BTECH

Vignan’s Nirula in Guntur is a premier institution known for quality education and innovation. Our B.Tech programs offer specialized courses in Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, and other core engineering streams, designed to equip students with practical skills, industry knowledge, and strong career opportunities.

MCA

The MCA program at Vignan’s Nirula is designed to build strong foundations in computer applications, programming, software development, and emerging technologies. With practical training, industry projects, and expert faculty, students gain the skills needed for careers in IT and software industries.

MBA

The MBA program at Vignan’s Nirula focuses on management excellence, leadership, and entrepreneurship. Offering specializations in Finance, Marketing, Human Resources, and Systems, the program prepares students for dynamic roles in corporate and business sectors.
